Idlewild

Casual With High End Drinks

March 15, 2019 Amateur Consumption

We had heard about the new cocktail bar in Noda, Idlewild, through Instagram and man were we not disappointed when we made it in! This speakeasy style bar is everything you want for your cocktails, while allowing you to come in jeans and a t-shirt. Due to North Carolina state laws they have to charge a membership fee. But don't worry, it's only $1 and let's you bring in 3 friends as often as you like.
