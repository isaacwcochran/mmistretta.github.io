Habanero Pineapple Hot Sauce

February 12, 2019 Amateur Consumption

We have been asked by a few people about our recipe for our Habanero Pineapple Hot Sauce, so here it is!

Ingredients:

  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 4 cups grilled pineapple
  • 3 jalapenos with seeds and veins in tact
  • 1 Serrano pepper with seeds and veins in tact
  • 2 habaneros deseeded and deveined
  • About a cup of Apple cider Vinegar
  • 1 Tb brown sugar
  • 1 onion
  • salt and pepper

Throw everything in a high speed blender like a Vitamix. Simmer over Medium heat for about 30 minutes. This will combine the flavors and lessen the harshness of the pure heat.
