The Butcher & Wine Bar

Meat Market and Wine Bar in One

November 14, 2019 Amateur Consumption

We recently made our way in the newest location of the NY Butcher Shoppe in Dilworth, here in Charlotte. Walking in, you immediately see all of the fresh meat to your right. From steaks to bone in pork shops and even crab cakes, the Butcher has everything you may need to cook a nice meal.

In the middle, there are shelves with dry goods, and towards the left some fridges with to go snacks, cheese, and more.

The Butcher has staff with vast experience in the restaurant industry and provides many ready meals to take home. These meals are cooked in house in the same kitchen as the food available at the wine bar. These meals can be found in the same fridges as the cheese.

Moving towards the back of the space there is a vast selection of wine, appropriately a majority is geared towards pairing with steak. Finally, behind that is the wine bar area. This is the first location of the NY Butcher Shoppe to have a wine bar as part of the location.

We sat down at the bar and decided to get some truffle fries and a bottle of Orin Swift Abstract. The fries were some of the best I have ever had with an amazing aioli! In addition, we had the chance to taste the philly cheesesteak, the scallops, and the hummus.

We ended up enjoying our time so much we stayed longer than expected and also ordered a steak flight! The steak flight is exactly what you would image, like a wine flight, but with steak. The selections are chosen by the chef and all 3 blew us away. The food offerings range from $6 to $18 and glasses of wine start at $6.

The Butcher also has a nice lounge area outside for when the weather is nice.

We highly recommend checking this place to grab a steak and bottle of wine for a nice night in or sit at the bar and make a Saturday of it by grabbing a snack and a drink!

Pro Tip: Find a bottle just above or at $40 and avoid paying a corkage fee to drink it at the bar.

Wine Club: Wine Club membership is $39.99/month and you recieve 2 bottles of wine hand picked by the Executive Chef and a Representative from their distributors. Wine selections include 1 white and 1 red each month and will have a common theme. In addition as a club member you will receive 10% all wine purchases.

Specials: Every Saturday various smoked meats get sold that have been smoked that day or the previous day.
