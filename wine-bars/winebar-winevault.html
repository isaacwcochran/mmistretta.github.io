Wine Vault

In the University Area

February 11, 2019 Amateur Consumption

Note: This article was first posted on August 15, 2018 here

Walking into The Wine Vault you can see it has a distinctive feel different from your typical wine bars in Charlotte. The patio is casual and filled with people, while the inside has couches and art work displayed. The atmosphere reminds me of a nice coffee shop inviting you to relax with friends or get some work done using the free WiFi. There is plenty of space, making it great for groups. I came with my husband and 2 friends and there was plenty of options for seating.

This place is made for wine snobs as well as those who just like to drink. Wine bottles ranging from $20 up to $100+ fill the space with staff ready to help. The price for beer is low at $4 for drafts. Prices for a glass of wine vary a bit more from $7-11 and while there is no kitchen at the Wine Vault, it is surrounded by restaurants at the University Boardwalk (p.s. outside food can be brought in). This wine bar is a great go-to place in the University Area for great wine and hanging out with friends!

Location and hours:
9009 J M Keynes Dr, Suite 1
Charlotte, NC 28262
  • Monday-Thursday 10am-11pm
  • Friday-Saturday 10am-12am
  • Sunday 1pm-11pm
Weekly Tasting Time: Wednesday 7PM – 8PM for $5 for 6 wines, sometimes up to 10 wines to taste

Who To Bring: Wine Vault is suiting from anything from a date to having family in town to a work meeting. The atmosphere is relaxed, casual, and non-intimidating.

Pro Tip: $Come with friends and get a bottle! You’ll save some money and have more options.

Events: Live music on Thursdays, Fridays, Saturdays, and Sundays. Weekly tastings on Wednesdays. Check Facebook for most up to date list of events.

Need to Know:The Wine Vault puts on a Wine Festival in the Spring called University Wine Fest. Be on the lookout for information on the event for 2019.

My Wine Bottle Selections: Txakoli ($20) and Valpolicella ($20)
